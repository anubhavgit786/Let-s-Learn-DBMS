1. A system containing multiple Hospitals, each with a unique identifier, name, and location.
2. Each Hospital has multiple Departments, and each Department can be staffed by multiple Doctors.
3. one department can be in many hospitals
4. Each Doctor can work in multiple hospitals and have multiple Patients assigned.
5. one doctor can work in multiple departments.
6. one doctor can work in multiple hospitals.
7. Patients are registered in the system with their unique identifier, name, and insurance details.
8. Each Patient's visit records to different Hospitals, their medical tests, prescriptions, and billing details are tracked.
9. The insurance details, claims, and settlements for each Patient are also maintained in the system.
10. An emergency service module that includes ambulances and their locations, dispatch records(start time, end time), and patient transport details.
