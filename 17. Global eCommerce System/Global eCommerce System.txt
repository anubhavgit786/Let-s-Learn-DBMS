Global eCommerce System
Use Cases
1. Global platform that operates in multiple Countries. Each Country has specific rules and tax rates.
2. Sellers register on the platform. Each Seller has a unique identifier and can operate in multiple Countries.
3. Each Seller lists multiple Products with different pricing, tax rates, and shipping rules for each Country.
4. Customers are registered in the platform from various Countries.
5. Customers can place Orders containing multiple Products from multiple Sellers. The Order tracks the pricing, taxes, shipping details, and status (placed, shipped, delivered, returned) for each Product.
6. A customer review and rating system for each product and seller, visible to all users.
7. A returns and refunds management system, with different policies for each Country and Seller.
8. A loyalty and rewards program with different tiers and benefits, and a points tracking system for each Customer.
